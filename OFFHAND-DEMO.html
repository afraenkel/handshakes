Project OFFHAND: Predicting gestures with Android Watch¶

  • Watches reduce friction, allowing for seamless user-engagement.
  • Watches are more likely to belong to the identity using the watch than a phone/computer.

Product Ideas:¶

  • Fast, personal gesture-based passwords. Paying with your phone? Have the usual password input replaced with a gesture when in range of the watch.
  • Easy gesture-based user input. Report an accident on waze with a flick of the wrist, instead of texting and driving.

Current App:¶

We recorded 4 different gestures:

  • Handshake,
  • Fistbump,
  • Slap-shake,
  • Hi Five

and used accelerometer data from an Android watch to recognize those gestures.

  • A very rough version is already installed on our Android Watch. . .
